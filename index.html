Hi, welcome to my portfolio page! I thought I would keep it straight forward and just show you what I worked on during my half year with Dark Tech.

Within Dark Tech, me and some friends worked on a controlling wearable product. I was in charge of many of the major decisions and designs of the project. Here is a global list of my contributions:

  • Design and fabrication of all 3 primary prototypes of the project.
  • Designing all needed hardware connections within the project.
  • Multiple custom prototype circuit boards.
  • All onscreen graphics.
  • First version of slot machine code for Society 5.0 expo.
  • Hardware interfacing code.
  • Consulting for design of program structure and layout.

It has been a fun project so here are some pictures of the end result!

This is the product I helped develop. You are supposed to wear it around your arm. I made a electrically actuated locking mechanism that automatically locks it shut. How fun! Now you can't escape :)



these are initial wireframes of the different screens I made. Of course in the actual version many of the elements that you see are placed programmatically because they are interactable.

These elements are all inspired by the super mario sprites used on the nintendo DS. Some of the fonts that we used were also from there.

I designed the models in FreeCAD and printed them with PLA plastic.



In total there were 3 complete design iterations of the product.

I also designed the electronics myself. It includes connections to all external interfaces and a failsave latching system for the arm strap.



The enclosure was very small for all the different added functions. I was able to solder everything in place using dupont connectors.
